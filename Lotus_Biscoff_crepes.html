Lotus Biscoff Crepes

Ingredients

For The Crepes

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 1/2 cups milk
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter
  • 1 tablespoon granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • A pinch of salt

For The Toppings

  • Grated white chocolate
  • Fresh berries
  • Melted Lotus Biscoff Spread
  • Additional crushed Lotus Biscoff cookies

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Crepe Batter. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the milk, melted butter and eggs until smooth.
  2. Combine the flour, salt and sugar and whisk until the batter is smooth and free of lumps.
  3. Let the batter rest for about 5-10 minutes to allow the flour to fully absorb the liquids.
  4. Cook the Crepes. Heat a non-stick skillet or crepe pan over medium heat. Lightly grease with butter or cooking spray.
  5. Pour about 1/4 cup of the batter into the pan, swirling it around to coat the bottom evenly.
  6. Cook for about 1-2 minutes until the edges start to lift and the bottom is golden brown. Flip the crepe and cook for another 1-2 minutes.
  7. Repeat with the remaining batter, stacking the cooked crepes on a plate and covering them with a clean kitchen towel to keep warm.
  8. Assemble the Crepes. Spread a generous amount of the Lotus Biscoff filling over one side of each crepe.
  9. Fold the crepes into quarters or roll them up.
  10. Garnish with crushed Lotus Biscoff cookies, whipped cream, fresh berries, or any other desired toppings.
  11. Serve the crepes warm, with additional toppings as desired. Enjoy the delightful combination of crepes and Lotus Biscoff!
