Vegetable Sandwich

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of your favorite bread
  • 1/2 large avocado
  • 1 small roma tomato
  • Red onion
  • Cucumber
  • Small handful baby spinach
  • Favorite hummus or mustard

Instructions

  1. Lightly toast your bread
  2. Meanwhile, slice tomato, cucumber, and onion.
  3. With a fork, mash avocado on one slice of toasted bread.
  4. On the other slice, spread mustard
  5. Layer your veggies between the two slices, place halves together
  6. Enjoy!
