Kinder Pancakes - For 6-8 pancakes

Ingredients

  • 160ml low fat milk
  • 170g self raising flour
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tbsp vanilla sugar
  • 4 kinder maxi bars (chopped up)
  • 2 tbsp butter

For toppings

  • 6 kinder maxi bars (melted)
  • 1 kinder bueno bar (chopped up)
  • 2 kinder bars (chopped up)
  • handful fresh raspberries

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl whisk together, all the pancake ingredients (except the butter), until just combined.
  2. In a large pan melt a little of the butter and add 2 tbsp of batter onto the pan. 2 tbsp = 1 pancake. Depending on the size of the pancakes this batter should make between 6-8.
  3. Once the air bubbles are forming on the uncooked side of the pancake, you know it is time to flip it.
  4. Cook for another minute or 2 on the uncooked side.
  5. Melt 6 of the kinder chocolate bars in the microwave until melted.
  6. Chop remaining 2 bars into pieces and break up the kinder bueno too.
  7. Stack the pancakes up and pour the melted chocolate over the top also adding the rest of the toppings.
  8. Serve and enjoy!
