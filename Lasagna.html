Lasagna

Ingredients

For The Meat Sauce

  • 2 lbs halal mince beef or mutton
  • 1 qt beef stock cube
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 tbsp plain flour
  • 2 diced onions
  • 250 g mix veg
  • 2 crushed garlic cloves
  • 3 tbsp tomato purée
  • 800 g chopped tomatoes
  • 1 tbsp chopped thyme

For The White Sauce

  • 50 g butter
  • 50 g plain flour
  • 1 l hot milk
  • ½ grated onion
  • 3 cloves
  • 50 g cheese, finely grated
  • 2 pinches salt and ground black pepper

For The Lasagne

  • 12 lasagne sheets
  • 100 g grated mature cheddar
  • 50 g grated mozzarella

Instructions

  1. First, infuse the milk to make the béchamel sauce. Put the 1-litre milk, 1 thickly sliced onion and 3 cloves into a large saucepan and bring very gently just up to the boil. Turn off the heat and set aside for 1 hr to infuse.
  2. Heat a large frying pan until hot and add the oil. Cook the mince until brown all over (about 10 minutes). Remove from the heat and transfer to a plate.
  3. Add the onion, mixed veg and garlic to the pan and cook until softened. Return the meat to the pan and stir in the flour. And then add the beef stock cube and bring to the boil. Finally add in tomato purée and thyme, then stir well.
  4. Stir in the canned tomatoes. Bring to the boil again, cover and cook for 1 hr, stirring occasionally to stop the bottom catching.
  5. Uncover and gently simmer for another 30 mins until the meat is tender and saucy. Taste and season.
  6. Preheat the oven to180C/356F/Gas 4.
  7. Strain the milk through a fine sieve into a jug. Melt the butter in a saucepan. Add the flour and cook over the heat for one minute. Gradually whisk in the hot milk, whisking until thickened.
  8. Add the cheese and season well with salt and pepper.
  9. Soften the lasagna sheets in boiling water - usually takes 10 minutes
  10. Put a small layer of the meat sauce in the base of the oven tray. Spoon one-third of the white sauce on top and if you want, add a bit of cheese. Arrange one layer of lasagne sheets on top, breaking them if need be to fill the cracks.
  11. Repeat this till you have three layers. Finish off with the final layer of white sauce and top with cheese. Use bother cheddar cheese for taste and mozzarella for the stretchiness.
  12. Place in the oven and bake for 45 mins until bubbling, and crisp on top. The pasta sheets should not be plasticky.
  13. Let it sit for 10 minutes before serving.
  14. Enjoy!
