Belgium Waffles

Ingredients

  • 2 cups (500ml) all-purpose flour
  • 2 tsp (10ml) baking powder
  • 1 tsp (5ml) bicarbonate of soda
  • 1/2 tsp (2ml) salt
  • 1 2/3 cups (400ml) milk
  • 1/2 cup (125ml) plain yoghurt
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/3 cup (75ml) vegetable oil
  • 1 tsp (5ml) vanilla
  • 60g Nutella - 1 heaped teaspoon (15g) per portion

Toppings

  • Sliced strawberries

Instructions

  1. In a bowl whisk together flour, baking powder, bicarbonate of soda and salt. In a separate bowl, whisk together the milk, yogurt, eggs, oil, and vanilla. Stir this mixture into the flour mixture until everything is moist.
  2. Heat a waffle maker according to the manufacturer's directions. Pour about 1 cup (250 ml) batter into the waffle maker and cook waffles according to manufacturer's directions. This is usually about 3 to 5 minutes or until golden and crisp. Repeat this with the remaining batter.
  3. Garnish each waffle with strawberries or any other berries that you want and 1 tsp. (15g) of Nutella.
  4. Enjoy!
