Peri Peri Chicken Wrap

Ingredients

  • 1 chicken breast â€“ diced.
  • Tsp of olive oil or low calorie cooking spray.
  • 1 tortilla wrap.
  • 3 tbsp Peri Peri sauce
  • 15g of grated cheddar (light optional).
  • Tbsp of Mayo
  • Squirt of ketchup.
  • 5 cherry tomatoes - cut in half.
  • A handful of salad.

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a non-stick pan and add chicken. Fry until cooked and then add peri peri sauce. Stir well and continue for 2-3 mins.
  2. Heat wrap in microwave for 20-30 seconds. Spread mayo onto wrap using the back of a spoon.
  3. Add cooked chicken to wrap and then add cheese. You can microwave the wrap at this point to get extra melted cheese. Add a squirt of ketchup, salad and tomatoes and wrap it up!
  4. Enjoy!
